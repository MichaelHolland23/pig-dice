Pig Dice

Here's how to play Pig Dice:


The goal is to reach one hundred.

Begin by rolling the Pig die.

You may roll as many times as you want, but if you roll a 1, your score for that round is nullified and it is your opponent's turn.

At any point of your round you may choose to "add to total" and your turn ends but your score is saved to your total! The winner of the game goes 2nd in the next game.
